Feature 1: Merchant or Utility Bill Payment with Cashback

User Story:

As a customer, I want to pay any merchant or utility bill so that I can fulfill my financial obligations and potentially receive cashback based on my transaction history.

Acceptance Criteria:

  1. The payment option is in the left navbar.
  2. The payment begins when the customer clicks the “payment” button.
  3. It contains “account from” and “account to”.
  4. When initiating a payment to any merchant or utility, a 1% service charge is deducted from the balance.
  5. The service charge must be at least 5 tk, regardless of the transaction amount.
  6. If total payments for the month do not exceed 5000 tk, no cashback is applied.
  7. When total payments for the month exceed 5000 tk, a 20% cashback is applied on subsequent payments up to a maximum cashback of 1000 tk.
  8. When total payments for the month exceed 10000 tk, a 30% cashback is applied on subsequent payments up to a maximum cashback of 3000 tk.
  9. The cashback amount must not exceed 1000 tk if the total payments are between 5000 tk and 10000 tk, and must not exceed 3000 tk if the total payments exceed 10000 tk.
  10. Cashback is calculated immediately after each qualifying payment is made.
  11. The cashback amount is credited instantly after the transaction is completed.
  12. The customer can check by clicking “statement” option in the left navbar.

Feature 2: Loan Application and Repayment

User Story:

As a customer with a low balance, I want to apply for a loan so that I can manage my financial needs until I can repay the loan.

Acceptance Criteria:

  1. A loan application can be made if the balance is less than 100 tk.
  2. The maximum loan amount available is 20,000 tk.
  3. The loan must be repaid within 30 days from the initiation date to avoid interest charges.
  4. If repaid within 30 days, no interest will be applied.
  5. A daily interest rate of 1.8% will be applied to the remaining loan amount if not repaid within 30 days.
  6. The customer becomes eligible to apply for another loan after repaying 50% of the remaining loan amount.
